Spider Man

Background Story

Peter Parker, known as Spider Man, was a poor sick orphan. One day, he was bit by a radioactive spider and turned into the superhero we all know as Spider Man.

How He Joined the Avengers

One day, while watching for crime, he happened to run into Tony Stark, aka Iron Man. Iron Man then requested him to fight in the Civil War, and with Captain America's permission, he became apart of the Avengers.
