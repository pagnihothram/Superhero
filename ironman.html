Iron Man

Background Story

Tony Stark is the CEO of Stark Industries. He is very smart in robotics. His company manufactures military weapons. When he was demonstrating a new missle in Afghanistan, terrists attacked ad he was hostage. He built a suit in the prison and escaped. When he got home, he improved his design to become Iron Man.

How he joined the Avengers

When Loki, Thor's brother, steals the Tesseract (a cube that contains on of the Infinity stones) from a secret project, Iron Man teams up with Captain America and Black Widow to get it back and they corner Loki in Germany.
